Celestial Nexus Hangar Sync Support

Basic troubleshooting

  1. Open the official RSI My Hangar page and sign in directly with RSI.
  2. Reload the page after installing or updating the extension.
  3. Load or scroll through all pledge cards before scanning.
  4. Open the extension popup and select Scan current RSI hangar page.
  5. Open Celestial Nexus and select Fleet Profile in the top-left toolbar, then connect the extension.

The scanner may require an update if RSI changes its hangar-page markup.
